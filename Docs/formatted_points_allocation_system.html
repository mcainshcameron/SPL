Points Allocation Overview:

This points allocation system is designed to encourage player participation, performance, sportsmanship, and referrals. The varied point allocations across match types and roles are meant to address the specific demands and complexities of each game format. Here's a simplified breakdown:

Participation Points:

Earned simply by playing.

Points increase with game size, promoting participation in larger matches.

5-a-side: 4 points

7-a-side: 5 points

11-a-side: 6 points

Goal Points:

Reflects scoring difficulty in each game type.

5-a-side: 1 point/goal

7-a-side: 1 point/goal

11-a-side: 2 points/goal

Own Goal Deductions:

A uniform penalty, given the equal impact of own goals.

All match types: -2 points/own goal

SPL Bonus Points:

Earned by being voted for the best goal, save, or assist.

5-a-side: 3 points

7-a-side: 3 points

11-a-side: 5 points

MVP Points:

Earned by being voted Most Valuable Player.

5-a-side: 3 points

7-a-side: 3 points

11-a-side: 5 points

Friend Referral Points:

Larger games need more players, so referrals for bigger matches earn more.

5-a-side: 3 points/referral

7-a-side: 4 points/referral

11-a-side: 5 points/referral

Penalty Deductions:

Higher deductions for larger games to reflect greater potential disruptions.

5-a-side: -3 points

7-a-side: -4 points

11-a-side: -5 points

Goalkeeper Points:

Constant across games, recognizing goalkeepers' unique role.

All match types: 1 point

Defensive Score Points:

Points are awarded based on team's defensive performance, with adjustments for individual roles.

5-a-side: 10 points (Outfield players receive half)

7-a-side: 10 points (Offensive players get none)

11-a-side: 8 points (Midfielders get half, Forwards get none)

Midfield Score:

In 11-a-side games, the score derives from goal difference. Forwards receive only half.

Match Roles:

Custom roles for each game size ensure role-specific rewards.

5-a-side: Goalkeeper, Outfield

7-a-side: Goalkeeper, Defensive, Offensive

11-a-side: Goalkeeper, Defender, Midfielder, Forward

In essence, this points system is tailored to echo the intricacies of each game format, fostering good play, collaboration, and dedication, while also discouraging negative behaviors. The goal is to ensure a fair reward system relative to the unique challenges of each game.
