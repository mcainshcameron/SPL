Points Allocation System Overview

This system encourages player participation, performance, sportsmanship, and referrals by awarding or deducting points. Different games have unique point values based on their demands.

1. Participation Points

Earned just for playing:

  • 5-a-side: 4 points
  • 7-a-side: 5 points
  • 11-a-side: 6 points

2. Game Outcome Points

Based on team results:

  • Win: All match types: 3 points
  • Draw: All match types: 1 point
  • Loss: All match types: 0 points (none awarded)

3. Goal Points

For each goal scored:

  • 5-a-side: 1 point/goal
  • 7-a-side: 1 point/goal
  • 11-a-side: 2 points/goal

4. Own Goal Points

Deductions for scoring against your team:

  • All match types: -2 points/own goal

5. SPL Bonus Points

Special achievement rewards:

  • 5-a-side: 3 points
  • 7-a-side: 3 points
  • 11-a-side: 5 points

6. MVP Points

For being the Most Valuable Player:

  • 5-a-side: 3 points
  • 7-a-side: 3 points
  • 11-a-side: 5 points

7. Friend Referral Points

For introducing new players:

  • 5-a-side: 3 points
  • 7-a-side: 4 points
  • 11-a-side: 5 points

8. Penalty Points

Deductions for misconducts:

  • 5-a-side: -3 points
  • 7-a-side: -4 points
  • 11-a-side: -5 points

9. Position-Specific Points

  • Goalkeeper Bonus: All match types: 1 point
  • Defensive Score Points: Based on goals conceded and player's position.
    • Base Defensive Score:
      • 5-a-side: 10 points
      • 7-a-side: 10 points
      • 11-a-side: 8 points
    • Scores start with the base value and may decrease by goals conceded. Full scores for 'Goalkeeper', 'Defender', or 'Defensive' players. Half scores for 'Midfielder' or 'Outfield'. None for 'Forward' and 'Offensive'. Minimum score is 0.
  • Midfield Score Points: Based on goal difference.
    • Full goal difference for 'Midfielder', 'Offensive', or 'Outfield'. Half for 'Forward'. No points for other positions.
